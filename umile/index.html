Background

Umile Background

Welcome to UMILE Group of companies and we would like to share with you our aspirations and vision of our company in year 2020. Our core business is our unique technology and ability to integrate products and systems and take responsibility in developing Malaysia whilst doing our business. UMILE Group is a company with great potential to grow in strategic areas, especially the ones prescribed by the Government of Malaysia to promote nation building and development of qualified human capital.


At UMILE, our employees and partners are the key to everything we do and a critical factor in order to implement the strategy and reach our strategic financial goals. Technology is constantly changing and we must naturally move along the exciting journey to the technology of the future. Malaysia, as our home nation shall be the impetus of our evolution, and as a wholly-owned Bumiputera company, UMILE shall stay on top of technological advancement and take responsibility to lead the development and produce new talents in the global market. There is no doubt our corporate responsibility is to fulfill our stakeholders’ financial targets, more importantly we have clearly defined values, which makes us a better employer for current and future employees. We are a company distinguished by professionalism, openness, cooperation and mutual respect. We are the natural choice for talented people who are looking to grow and want to embrace change in a stimulating working environment.
